How much do we really spend on Valentines Day? Well, According to "How Much We'll Spend On Valentine's Day" (2015), "The average person celebrating Valentine's Day this Saturday plans to shell out $142.31 for candy, flowers, jewelry and a special night out, up from $133.91 last year". And of course, we each have that special someone that deserves our hard earned income. Some choose to invest in their significant other, some see fit to remain in good standings with friends and family or those in their children's academic circles, while others just pamper their little furry friends.

Check out these statistics.

$142.31: Amount the average person expects to spend

$87.94: Average amount people expect to spend on significant others

$26.26: How much people plan to spend on other friends and family

$6.30: Amount people intend to spend on children's classmates and teachers

$5.28: How much celebrants plan to spend on pets
